Hiroshima

The original top target for the A-Bomb was a city called Kokura. However, due to cloudy weather, Hiroshima was decided on instead. The nickname of the weapon used was Little Boy and the bomber was a B-29 named Enola Gay. The bomb was dropped at 8:15 Japan time on August 6th.

Nagasaki

Despite the horrible distruction caused by the bombing of Hiroshima, the Japanese high command did not accept the American's offer of unconditional surrender. As such, another Atomic Bomb, called Fat Man was dropped three days later on the port city of Nagasaki.

Although the Fat Man was greater than that of Little Boy, the differing geography between Hirsoshima and Nagasaki caused it do do less damage.

Neverless, the combination of the two atomic bombings with the Soviet rumor of declaration of way compelled the Japanese high command to finally surrender on the 12th.
